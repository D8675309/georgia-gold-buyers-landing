About Georgia Gold Buyers

Family-owned. Community-trusted. Serving Georgia since 2012.

Our Story

Georgia Gold Buyers was founded in 2012 with a simple mission: give people a trustworthy, transparent place to sell their gold, silver, and jewelry for a fair price.

What started as a single location has grown into a family-operated business with locations in McDonough and Valdosta, Georgia. Over the years, we've expanded from buying precious metals to also purchasing luxury watches, designer handbags, fine jewelry, and estate collections.

We've built our reputation one customer at a time — through honest evaluations, competitive payouts, and a no-pressure environment where people feel comfortable. Our 5-star Google rating with over 84 reviews isn't something we bought or gamed. It's the result of treating every person who walks through our door like family.

Today, we serve customers from across Georgia and North Florida, and many drive over an hour to visit us because they know they'll get a fair deal and a respectful experience.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

Honesty First

Every item is tested and evaluated right in front of you. We explain our process, show you the numbers, and let you decide. No tricks, no pressure.

Fair Pricing

We base our offers on live market prices and consistently pay more than pawn shops. Bring any competitor's offer and we'll do our best to beat it.

Community Roots

We're not a chain or a franchise. We live and work in the same communities we serve — McDonough and Valdosta.

Family Business

Georgia Gold Buyers is owned and operated by the Woodall family. When you do business with us, you're dealing directly with the owners.

Meet Our Team

The people behind Georgia Gold Buyers — dedicated to giving you the best experience and the best price.

Gadson Woodall

Owner & Precious Metals Specialist

Gadson Woodall is the founder and operator of Georgia Gold Buyers, with locations in Valdosta and McDonough, Georgia. With over a decade of experience in the precious metals industry, Gadson specializes in evaluating and purchasing gold, silver, and platinum items — including jewelry, coins, bullion, and scrap metal.

A dedicated entrepreneur, he built the business to serve as a trustworthy and discreet option for individuals looking to get the most value for their precious items. Beyond gold buying, Gadson brings a background in electronics repair, digital marketing, and small business development.

Gad Woodall

Owner & Precious Metals Specialist

Gad Woodall is both an owner of Georgia Gold Buyers and the trusted expert at our McDonough location. With years of hands-on experience in the gold and precious metals industry, Gad is known for his integrity, fair pricing, and deep knowledge of the market.

He provides every customer with a professional, pressure-free experience — whether they're selling gold jewelry, coins, bullion, or other valuable items. A dedicated member of the McDonough community, Gad takes pride in offering honest evaluations and top-dollar payouts.

Glenn Paschal

General Manager

Glenn Paschal is the General Manager of Georgia Gold Buyers and has been a key part of the team since 2012. With over a decade of experience in retail operations, customer service, and precious metals, Glenn plays a vital role in ensuring smooth daily operations and upholding the company's reputation for trust and transparency.

He's known for his integrity, professionalism, and commitment to delivering exceptional customer experiences. Outside of work, Glenn enjoys spending time with his family and giving back to the local community.

Jessica Woodall

Designer Jewelry & Luxury Handbag Specialist

Jessica Woodall is Georgia Gold Buyers' in-house expert on designer jewelry and luxury handbags. With a keen eye for high fashion and years of hands-on experience, Jessica specializes in identifying and appraising top-tier brands — from David Yurman and Tiffany & Co. to Chanel, Louis Vuitton, and more.
